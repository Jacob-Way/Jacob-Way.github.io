Background

This paper discusses the problem of hearing the shape of a drum. An intuitive version of the question is: drum heads of different sizes and shapes make a different noise when you hit them, but when do two differently shaped drums make the same noise? In this case, the authors are studying elliptical drums which are nearly circular, and show that each elliptical drum has a unique sound.
